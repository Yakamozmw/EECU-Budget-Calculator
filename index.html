Wise-up Budget Calculator

Welcome

The EECU Wise-Up Budget Calculator is a helpful tool designed to teach users how to manage their money in a realistic and practical way. Created by Educational Employees Credit Union (EECU), the calculator allows users to explore different career paths, estimate their income, and plan out monthly expenses such as housing, transportation, food, and entertainment. By simulating real-life financial decisions, it helps users understand how budgeting works and why it’s important to make smart choices with money.

The Wise-Up Budget Calculator provides a hands-on way to practice financial planning and responsibility. It encourages users to think about balancing needs versus wants while staying within a realistic budget based on their chosen career. This tool helps build important life skills and promotes awareness of how everyday financial decisions can impact long-term goals. Overall, it serves as a valuable resource for developing strong money management habits.
